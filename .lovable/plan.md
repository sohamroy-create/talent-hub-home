

# JobletAI Homepage

A corporate, professional homepage in blue & white with 6 content sections, a header, footer, and floating search bar.

## Header
- Placeholder navigation bar with JobletAI logo/brand name on the left and placeholder nav items + Login/Signup buttons on the right
- Sticky header with clean white background and subtle shadow

## Section 1 – Hero Image
- Full-width abstract AI-themed visual using CSS gradients and geometric patterns (blues, whites, subtle tech elements)
- Overlaid hero text welcoming users to JobletAI

## Floating Search Bar
- Search bar spanning 80% width, positioned to overlap bottom of Section 1 and top of Section 2
- Elevated with shadow for a floating effect
- Search icon and placeholder text

## Section 2 – Categories
- Grid of 14 clickable category boxes: AI, IT Services, Healthcare, Manufacturing & Production, Supply Chain, Infrastructure, Transport, Real Estate, Finance & Accounting, Consulting, Sales & Marketing, Hospitality, Media & Entertainment, Education
- Each box has an icon and label, with hover effects
- Links are placeholder (configurable later)

## Section 3 – Social Proof & Features (fits in viewport with Sections 1-2)
- **Left half – "Why JobletAI"**: Left 30% shows "Why JobletAI" in vibrant gradient text. Right 70% has horizontally scrollable stat cards (5678 Recruiters, 38 Countries, 3001 Cities, 1243 Companies)
- **Right half – Features**: Left 30% alternates between "Upcoming Features" and "New Features" labels in vibrant colors. Right 70% shows scrollable feature cards that change based on the active label:
  - *Upcoming*: ApplyAI (links to Section 6), Interview AI (links to Section 6), Resume Scorer, Salary Calculator
  - *New*: AdvertiseAI (links to Section 5), Job Posting Optimiser (links to Section 5), Blogs (links to Section 5)

## Section 4 – Trusted By Industry Leaders
- Narrow banner section with "Trusted by Industry Leaders" heading
- 10 transparent square logo slots in a continuously scrolling marquee (right-to-left), showing 5 at a time
- Placeholder slots ready for logo uploads later

## Section 5 – Jobs, Blogs, AdvertiseAI
- Three equal cards side by side, each with hover pop-up effect:
  - **Jobs**: "Post a Job" (clickable) with subtext "Source talent, Globally" + "Look for a job" (clickable) with subtext "Look for jobs, Globally"
  - **Blogs**: "Post a Blog" (clickable) with subtext "Become a thought leader" + "Look for a Blog" with subtext "Look for Industry insights"
  - **AdvertiseAI**: "Making your Jobs" with subtext "Reach the correct candidates"
- All links are placeholder (configurable later)

## Section 6 – ApplyAI, InterviewAI, Suggestions
- Three equal cards side by side with hover pop-up effect:
  - **ApplyAI**: "Applying for a job just got 83% easier!" / Subtext with "27 days to launch" in bold
  - **InterviewAI**: "Give AI Interviews without any hassle!" / Subtext with "43 days to launch" in bold
  - **Suggestions**: "Let us know what feature you want!" / "And we will name the feature after you!"

## Footer
- Simple corporate footer with JobletAI branding, placeholder links, and copyright

## Design Notes
- Blue & white corporate color scheme throughout
- Sections 1–3 designed to fit within one desktop viewport (no scroll needed)
- Smooth scroll anchoring for internal "Learn More" links between sections
- Responsive layout with clean typography and consistent spacing
